BlockVote

"A fully decentralized voting system built on the Ethereum blockchain. Every vote is stored permanently on-chain via a Solidity smart contract — immutable, transparent, and publicly verifiable on Etherscan. No backend server. No database. The blockchain is the backend."

Problem Statement

Traditional voting systems face critical challenges: centralized control creates single points of failure, results can be tampered with, and voters have no way to independently verify their vote was counted. Existing e-voting solutions either rely on trusted third parties or require expensive infrastructure. There is a need for a transparent, tamper-proof, and decentralized voting mechanism accessible to anyone.

Existing Solutions & Gaps

Estonia's e-voting, Voatz, and Follow My Vote rely on centralized backends or expensive smart contract deployments on mainnet. Most require paid infrastructure or have closed-source code that cannot be independently audited. Our solution is fully open-source, free to deploy, and runs on a public testnet.

Our Innovation

BlockVote uses a Solidity smart contract as the entire backend — no Node.js server, no database, no paid APIs. Wallet-based authentication replaces username/password. One wallet address = one vote, enforced permanently by the contract's mapping(address => bool) structure.

Security Rules (require checks)

onlyAdmin modifier — only deployer can create elections or add candidates
mapping(address ⇒ bool) — one vote per wallet, enforced on-chain
Timestamp checks — cannot vote before start or after deadline
Candidate validation — rejects invalid candidate IDs

How It Works

01

Connect Wallet

User connects MetaMask. Wallet address becomes their unique voter identity.

02

View Elections

Active elections fetched live from the blockchain. No API call — direct contract read.

03

Cast Vote

Vote signed by MetaMask, sent as blockchain transaction. Contract checks — no double voting.

04

Live Results

Results fetched directly from contract. Verifiable on Etherscan by anyone.
